Common questions

Which banks are supported?
Any of the 2,500+ banks across 29 European countries supported by Enable Banking, including Revolut, N26, Monzo, Wise, Millennium BCP, Santander, ING, BNP Paribas, and many more. Plus portfolio tracking for eToro and Binance. Personal accounts only.
Do I need to know how to code?
No coding needed. You need Docker installed, but after that the entire setup is done through a browser-based wizard — no terminal commands beyond docker compose up -d.
Is my bank data safe?
Yes. Klartion uses read-only access via Enable Banking's regulated open banking API — it cannot move money or make any changes to your account. Your data goes directly to your machine and never passes through our servers.
What do I need to run Klartion?
A machine that can run Docker (Mac, Linux, Raspberry Pi, NAS, or a small VPS), a free Enable Banking account, and a Notion account with a database to sync into.
Can I use it on more than one machine?
Each licence key can be activated on up to 2 machines. If you move to a new machine, deactivate your current installation from within the app and reactivate on the new one.
Is there a hosted version?
Not yet, but it's coming. Join the waitlist in the pricing section to be notified when it launches. No Docker, no server — just connect your bank and Notion.
