Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What Klartion is

Klartion is a self-hosted application. You run it on your own machine. Your bank transaction data is fetched from Enable Banking and written directly to your Notion database. It never passes through our servers.

What we collect

When you purchase a licence, Lemon Squeezy collects your name and email address to process the payment and deliver your licence key. We receive your email address solely for support purposes.

When you activate your licence key, we record that an activation occurred. We do not store any personally identifiable information beyond your email address.

What we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to your bank transactions, account balances, Notion data, Enable Banking credentials, or SMTP passwords. All of this data stays on your machine.

Third-party services

Klartion integrates with the following third-party services on your behalf: Enable Banking (open banking API), Notion (database), and your chosen SMTP provider (email notifications). Their respective privacy policies apply to your use of those services.

Licence validation is handled by Lemon Squeezy. Their privacy policy is available at lemonsqueezy.com.

Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as your licence is active. You can request deletion at any time by emailing support@david-alves.com.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, email support@david-alves.com.
